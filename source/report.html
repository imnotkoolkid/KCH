Report Content

01

Overview

If someone has uploaded content that belongs to you (or includes any part of your original work) without permission, you may request a review and removal of your work whether it is the entire project or only a portion used within another project.

02

This Applies To

This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Full CSS files
  • Modified or partially edited versions of your work
  • Assets embedded within CSS (images, fonts, sounds, etc.)
  • Code included in scripts or clients
  • Textures, crosshairs, or other visual assets
  • Any portion of your original content used without proper authorization

If you believe your work has been used without permission, you may submit a removal request.

03

How to Submit

Please contact imnotkoolkid to submit your takedown request:

When submitting your report, please include:

  • A direct link to the content on our website
  • Proof that you are the original creator or rights holder
Incomplete, misleading, or false reports will be ignored.
04

What Happens Next

Once your request has been reviewed, appropriate action will be taken. This may include removing or modifying the reported content within a reasonable timeframe.
